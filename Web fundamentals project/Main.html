Fashion Should Not Cost The Earth

Fashion Farm is a non-profit Malaysian organization established in 2020 to advance sustainability in the fashion system through education, awareness, research and collaboration. We achieve this through events and programs such as Fashion Farm Week, Upclycling Workshops and Ecouture Sustainable Fashion Competition.
