We Need Your Support

Fashion Farm is working towards raising awareness on sustainable fashion.

Transaction successful! Thank you for the donation!

Your donation will help to:

· Help out less privileged community to obtain sufficient clothing.

Despite the numerous amount of clothing and textile waste, there is still a wide number of citizens struggling to wear decent clothes that protect and sustain their daily life. 50% of the collected funds go directly to Tzu Chi Foundation.

· Inspire people to think differently about our clothes

We create interactive resources, practical guides and innovative events to think differently about how we buy, care and dispose of our clothes.

· Promote ethical standards of conduct amongst industry and policymakers

We celebrate best practice and work to secure respect for the rights of garment workers and to strengthen environmental protection.
