PROGRAMS & EVENTS

FashionFarm is a dynamic sustainable fashion event comprising of a number of exciting activities for the students of Sunway Campus to participate in. This includes FashionFarm's Collection Drive, Ecouture Outfit Design Contest, and the Main Event!



FashionFarm Clothing Collection Drive

May 13th, 2022 - FashionFarm's Collection Drive is for those who have any pre-loved clothes they wish to donate. The FashionFarm Collection Drive's purpose is to reduce consumption, reuse and repurpose clothing by collection

Ecouture Sustainable Design Competition

April 30, 2022 - Throughout the years, more and more sustainable fashion brands are starting to catch the eyes of our public. Unleash your creativity and talent here by creating your own masterpiece.

FashionFarm Week

June 8, 2022 -The main FashionFarm event will feature exciting exhibitions and workshops with the intentions of advocating for sustainable fashion.

Rags to Reusables: Upcycling Workshop

June 9, 2022 - Our workshops are designed to engage everyone – at any age or levels – in the positive social and environmental impact your company is making, and why it is taking action. The workshops included are zipper pencil case workshop, scrunchies making workshop and circular drawstring bag workshop.
